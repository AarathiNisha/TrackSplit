About This App

This Expense Tracker is a simple web application that helps you manage your personal finances efficiently. You can add, view, filter, and delete your expenses by category, and keep track of your total spending.
Key Features:
  • Add and manage daily expenses with categories
  • Filter by category and track total amounts
  • User login/logout for data privacy
Purpose:

This app was created to help users gain better visibility into their spending habits and take control of their financial goals.
